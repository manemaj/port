Motion Control Robotic End-Effector

Designed a camera mount for a discarded Denso 6-axis industrial robot. Integrated a focus-pulling gearbox, DSLR camera, and rail system to create a motion control rig for filming. The hardest part was programming the old robot and making all the mechanical systems work together precisely. Modeled everything in CATIA V5 and got smooth point-to-point camera movements working with automated focus control.

Fidget Spinner - Early CAD/CAM Project

One of my first CAD/CAM projects. Designed and machined a fidget spinner in my garage using skateboard bearings. Learned how to work with CAD software, generate tool paths in CAM, and actually machine parts with proper tolerances and fitment. It wasn't perfect, but it worked and my friends thought it was pretty cool that I built it myself. Got me hooked on mechanical design.
